1. Information we collect

When you contact NT Auto Works or book a service, we collect the details you give us — your name, phone number, email address and information about your vehicle and the work you need. We only collect what we need to quote and carry out the job.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, prepare quotes, book and complete work, contact you about your vehicle, and keep a service record. We do not sell your information to anyone.

3. Sharing

We may share limited details with parts suppliers or sub-contractors strictly where it's needed to complete your job. We may also disclose information where required by New Zealand law.

4. Storage and security

Enquiries submitted through this website are delivered to us by email. We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and to retain it only as long as it's needed for servicing records and legal obligations.

5. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request that we correct it. Contact us using the details below.

About NT Auto Works

Your local mechanic, not a sales pitch

NT Auto Works is a Christchurch mechanical workshop built on a simple idea: tell people the truth about their car, charge a fair price, and do the job once. We service and repair all makes and models — daily drivers, work utes and the family wagon alike.

You'll deal with the people doing the work, get a straight answer about what's urgent and what can wait, and pick up a car that's been sorted properly.
